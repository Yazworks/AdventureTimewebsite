About BMO

BMO is a living game console and one of the most recognisable characters from the world of Ooo. This page uses separate animated GLB files to show different BMO moods and actions.

The interaction buttons use JavaScript to swap models, replay animation, move the camera, and toggle wireframe mode for model inspection.
