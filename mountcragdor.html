About the Enchiridion

The Enchiridion is one of the most powerful relics in all of Ooo. Its gemstones, when collected, can tear open portals to different timelines and alternative dimensions. The Key-Per helps guard the Enchiridion at the top of Mount Cragdor, where only true heroes may be able to harness the power and scripture hidden within it. It is said only true heroes can open it and discover its wisdom; would you be willing to give it a try? Shaking it may result in spells falling out, so be mindful not to allow the many witches and wizards of Ooo to get their hands on such power. It is said that in the Candy Kingdom, there is a well-renowned magician no one suspects...
